Film Profitability Analysis

Tools used: Python, pandas, matplotlib, SQL, SQLite

Dataset: TMDB Movies Dataset (Kaggle)

If you wanted to make a film, how you wanted it to perform is solely dependent on the budget. If a big budget is present, the data shows the best option is an action/animated blockbuster because they have a bigger reach and franchise appeal as they appeal to multiple age ranges and demographics and in turn return a great profit. However if you had a small budget the better choice would be a comedy/thriller/drama as they have a greater ROI because they have a smaller budget and the profit scales nicely in accordance so they have a lower cost and in turn a lower risk.
